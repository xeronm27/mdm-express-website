Market Context

Why COD is Essential in Lebanon

Lebanon's unique economic environment makes COD not just preferred — but necessary.

🏦

Banking Crisis Impact

Since 2019, Lebanese banking restrictions have severely limited online payment capabilities. COD bypasses this entirely, enabling e-commerce to function where digital payments cannot.

💵

Cash Economy

Lebanon operates largely in cash — both LBP and USD. COD aligns perfectly with how Lebanese consumers manage their money day-to-day.

📱

High Digital Literacy

Lebanon has one of the highest social media penetration rates in the Arab world. Consumers discover and buy products online but pay in cash at the door.

📈

Resilient E-Commerce

Despite economic challenges, Lebanon's e-commerce sector is growing. Sellers who operate with a COD model are the ones succeeding and scaling.

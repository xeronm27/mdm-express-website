Market Context

Why COD Dominates E-Commerce in Libya

Understanding the Libyan market is essential for any e-commerce seller.

🏦

Limited Banking Access

Less than 30% of Libyan adults have active bank accounts. Credit card penetration is under 5%, making COD the only practical payment method for the vast majority of consumers.

📦

Inspect Before You Pay

Libyan consumers strongly prefer to inspect products before paying. COD builds the trust needed to convert first-time buyers — essential in a market where returns are common.

📱

Social Commerce Boom

Facebook and Instagram drive the majority of e-commerce sales in Libya. Products go viral fast — but buyers expect to pay on delivery, not in advance.

🚀

Untapped Market

Libya's e-commerce market is still in its early stages with massive growth potential. Early movers who build COD infrastructure now will dominate for years.

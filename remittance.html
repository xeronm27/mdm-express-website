How It Works

T+0 to T+7 — Every Single Week

From the moment your customer pays cash at the door to the moment funds hit your bank — every step is automatic, every cycle is exactly 7 days.

💵
Day 0 · T+0
COD Collected

Delivery agent hands over the parcel and collects cash at the door. POD photo uploaded instantly. Cash is logged to your dashboard in real time.

⚙️
Day 1–2 · T+1
Auto-Reconciliation

Every collected payment is matched to its shipment ID overnight. Returns and NDRs are deducted automatically — nothing manual, nothing missed.

🏦
Day 3–5 · T+3
Funds Processed

Net payable amount is calculated after deducting delivery fees and returns. Final figure locked and queued for bank transfer on Sunday.

✅
Day 7 · T+7
Payout Sent

Every Sunday your full weekly remittance arrives in your registered bank account. No follow-up needed. This cycle repeats every 7 days — without exception.

When exactly does my weekly payout arrive? +
Every Sunday. The cycle runs Monday to Sunday. All orders confirmed and reconciled by Friday are included in that Sunday's payout. Orders confirmed Saturday or Sunday roll into the following week's cycle.
What happens to returned or failed delivery orders? +
Returns and NDR-failed orders are deducted from your weekly remittance automatically. You'll see a full line-by-line breakdown in your dashboard before the payout is processed — no surprises and no disputes.
Is there a minimum payout threshold? +
No minimum threshold. Even if you have a slow week with a handful of orders, you still receive your full collected amount on Sunday. We never hold funds until a minimum is reached.
Can I get paid faster than weekly? +
High-volume merchants with strong track records may be eligible for accelerated payouts. Contact our team to discuss — eligibility is based on monthly remittance volume and account standing.

FAQ

Common Questions

Still unclear on something?

Our team is happy to walk you through the full process before you commit to anything.

Talk to Us →
How long does setup take? +
From signup to your first live order typically takes 48 hours. Store connection is instant; onboarding for sourcing and warehousing takes 1–2 business days depending on your product category.
Do I need to source products through MDM Express? +
No. You can bring your own products to our warehouse. Sourcing through us is optional but recommended — you get verified suppliers, QC inspection, and typically 25–35% lower unit costs.
What happens if a customer refuses the order on delivery? +
Returned orders come back to our warehouse at no extra cost. You're only charged for the delivery attempt. Our call center confirmation step is specifically designed to reduce refusals.
Can I sell in multiple countries at the same time? +
Yes. You can activate Libya, Iraq and Lebanon simultaneously from one dashboard. Inventory can be split or pooled across our fulfillment centers depending on your sales volumes.
When exactly do I receive my weekly payout? +
Payouts are processed every Thursday and typically arrive in your account within 24 hours. You can see the exact schedule and breakdown in your dashboard before the transfer happens.

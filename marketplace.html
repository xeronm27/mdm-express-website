FAQ

Common Questions

What is the minimum order quantity?
Every marketplace request requires a minimum of 50 pieces. This applies per product per order. There is no maximum limit.
How long does sourcing take?
Once you submit a request, MDM contacts the supplier and confirms stock within a few hours. Full sourcing and delivery to you takes 24–48 hours from confirmation.
Do I pay before or after the stock is confirmed?
Payment is made after MDM confirms stock availability and final pricing with the supplier. You will receive a confirmation with the exact cost before proceeding.
What is the difference between Dropshipping and Marketplace?
With Dropshipping you sell one item at a time — MDM delivers directly to your customer and you pay after delivery. With Marketplace, you buy stock in bulk (50+ pcs) from local Libyan suppliers and hold inventory yourself, then sell through your own channels.
Can I request a product that's not in the catalog?
Yes. If you can't find what you're looking for, contact your MDM account manager and we'll look for a suitable local supplier that matches your needs.

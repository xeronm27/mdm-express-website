What Happens After "Confirmed"

Every COD order follows a precise chain of custody — no gaps, no black boxes.

T+0

Order Confirmed

COD order confirmed by our call centre. Pick list auto-generated and sent to warehouse.

T+2 h

Get in Touch
How does MDM Express choose which carrier to use? +
Our routing engine scores each available carrier on delivery SLA history, real-time capacity, and destination zone — then assigns the best match automatically at manifest time.
What happens if a delivery attempt fails? +
NDR triggers automatically: our call centre contacts the customer to reschedule, correct the address, or arrange a return. You choose the max retry count in your dashboard settings.
When do I receive my COD remittance? +
Cash collected during the week is reconciled and transferred to your registered bank account every week. You can view the full breakdown in your remittance report at any time.
Can customers track their own orders? +
Yes — every shipped order generates a customer-facing tracking URL, sent via SMS at dispatch. The page updates in real time as the parcel moves through the network.

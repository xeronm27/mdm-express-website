Get in Touch
Do you handle delivery in all cities across Libya, Iraq, and Lebanon? +
Yes — we have active carrier coverage across all major cities in all three markets, including secondary cities and rural zones. Contact us to confirm coverage for a specific destination.
What happens when a delivery fails (NDR)? +
NDR triggers automatically. Our team contacts the customer to reschedule, correct the address, or arrange a return. You control the max retry count and escalation rules from your dashboard.
In which currencies do you collect COD? +
We collect in the local currency of each market — Libyan Dinar (LYD) in Libya, Iraqi Dinar (IQD) in Iraq, and Lebanese Pound (LBP) in Lebanon. Payouts are made weekly to your registered bank account.
Can my customers track their orders in real time? +
Every shipped order generates a customer-facing tracking link, sent via SMS at dispatch. The page updates live as the parcel moves through the network — no login required for the customer.

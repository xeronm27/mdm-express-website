Common Questions

About MDM Express

When was MDM Express founded?
MDM Express was founded in 2016 and has been operating in the MENA region for over 8 years, growing from a Libya-focused logistics provider to a multi-market COD e-commerce platform serving Libya, Iraq, and Lebanon.
Where is MDM Express headquartered?
MDM Express is headquartered at Creative Tower, Dubai, UAE, with an additional registered office at 71-75 Shelton Street, London, UK. Operations are run locally in Libya, Iraq, and Lebanon through in-country teams and warehouse networks.
How many sellers does MDM Express work with?
MDM Express works with over 9,000 active sellers across Libya and Iraq, with operations expanding into Lebanon. Sellers range from solo dropshippers to established e-commerce brands sourcing and fulfilling thousands of orders per month.
What makes MDM Express different from a standard courier?
MDM Express is not a courier — it is a full COD e-commerce operations partner. Beyond last-mile delivery, MDM handles product sourcing, warehousing, order confirmation via local call centers, cash collection, weekly seller payouts, and inventory financing. Sellers get one integrated system instead of managing five separate vendors.
Does MDM Express take equity or a share of my business?
No. MDM Express operates on a service fee model — we charge for the services we provide and do not take equity, shares, or a percentage of your profits. The sourcing advance is repaid from your COD revenue, not through equity.
Which markets does MDM Express currently serve?
MDM Express currently operates in Libya, Iraq, and Lebanon, with active expansion across the MENA region. New markets are being added as the infrastructure scales.

FAQ

Quick Answers

Need more detail?

Our operations team will walk you through pricing, minimums, and onboarding — usually within 24 hours.

Talk to Us
What are the storage fees? +
Storage is billed per pallet or cubic metre per month. Active SKUs that ship regularly get preferential rates — contact us for a tailored quote based on your volume and product mix.
Is there a minimum inventory requirement? +
No hard minimum — but to make the most of the pick-pack SLA we recommend maintaining at least 7 days of projected sales stock at all times.
How do I send inventory to your warehouse? +
You book an inbound shipment through your dashboard, print our routing labels, and ship to the nearest hub. Our receiving team checks in every unit on arrival — usually within 2 hours of delivery.
What happens to returned items? +
Returned items are inspected, graded (sellable / QC / damaged), and either restocked, held for review, or flagged for disposal per your instructions — all visible in your dashboard.

FAQ

Quick Answers.

Everything sellers ask before getting started.

Still have questions?

Our team is available 24/7. Reach out and we'll walk you through the right services for your business.

Talk to us →
Can I use one service without the others?
+
Yes — every service is fully modular. Most sellers start with Dropshipping or the Call Centre and expand as they grow. There's no obligation to use the full stack.
Which markets do you cover?
+
We're live in Libya, Iraq and Lebanon, with active expansion into the wider MENA region. Some services are market-specific — check each service page for coverage details.
How long does onboarding take?
+
Most accounts go live within 48–72 hours of submitting your application. Dropshipping sellers can often start selling on day one.
Do you handle returns and NDR?
+
Yes — NDR (non-delivery report) management and return processing are included as part of the Warehousing service. We reattempt delivery before marking a package returned.
How does the Sourcing Advance work?
+
The Advance is performance-based — we look at your order volume, delivery rate and history with MDM. Approved sellers receive inventory funding with no equity or collateral required. Repayment is deducted from future remittances.
What's the minimum order for the Marketplace?
+
The MDM Marketplace requires a minimum of 50 pieces per product. Orders are sourced from local Libyan suppliers and delivered within 24–48 hours.

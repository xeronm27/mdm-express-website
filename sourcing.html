Talk to a Sourcing Agent
How do I submit a sourcing request? +
Submit a brief through your MDM Express dashboard — product name, target price, quantity, and any specs. Alternatively, contact your account manager directly via WhatsApp. We'll respond with initial supplier options within 48 hours.
Is there a minimum order quantity (MOQ)? +
MOQ depends on the product and supplier — typically 50–200 units for China factory orders, lower for Turkey and UAE stock. We'll always tell you the MOQ upfront before you commit. Sample orders of 1–5 units are available for most categories.
Can I order samples before committing to a bulk order? +
Yes — we strongly recommend it. Sample orders are available for most products at 2–3× unit cost to cover the fixed shipping overhead. Samples arrive to our warehouse and can be forwarded to you directly for inspection before you place the bulk order.
What if the product I want isn't in your catalogue? +
Submit it anyway. Our team handles custom sourcing requests for products we've never sourced before. If it exists and can be manufactured or purchased at scale, we can find it — across China, Turkey, UAE, and local MENA markets.

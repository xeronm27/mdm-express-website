What exactly do I pay MDM after delivery? +
You pay the product cost plus MDM's service fees — which cover call center confirmation, warehouse fulfillment, and last-mile delivery. Everything above that is your profit, paid out to you weekly.
What happens if the customer refuses delivery? +
If an order is rejected at the door, you are not charged the product cost — only the relevant service fees already incurred. Our call center's 98% confirmation rate keeps rejection rates very low.
Can I sell any product from the MDM catalog? +
Yes — any product in stock at our Libya warehouse is available for dropshipping. You can submit requests for multiple products at the same time.
How long does delivery take in Libya? +
We offer next-day delivery to all major Libyan cities. Once confirmed by our call center, the order ships the same day and reaches the customer the next day.
When will Iraq and Lebanon go live? +
We are actively building the infrastructure needed. Exact launch dates will be announced on our website and social channels.

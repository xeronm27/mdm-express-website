How is the service priced? +
Per-confirmed-order pricing — you only pay when an agent successfully confirms a COD order. No flat fees, no setup costs, no hidden charges.
How fast do agents respond to new orders? +
Average answer time is under 30 seconds from order placement. Orders are queued and routed instantly to the next available agent.
What languages do your agents speak? +
Arabic (Gulf dialect), English, and French. The agent language is matched to the customer's shipping region automatically.
Can I listen to call recordings? +
Yes — all calls are recorded and available in your seller dashboard for quality review, training, and dispute resolution.

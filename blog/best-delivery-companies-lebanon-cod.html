Best Delivery Companies in Lebanon with COD Service 2026

MDM Express Team July 7, 2026 10 min read

Lebanon's market is small in geography but demanding in logistics. Mountain villages, congested Beirut traffic, regional economic variation, and the aftermath of the banking crisis make delivery partner selection critical. Choosing wrong means coverage gaps, cash sitting with a courier for weeks, and delivery rates that undermine your margins.

This is a current, honest assessment of the best COD delivery options for e-commerce sellers in Lebanon in 2026.

2. Aramex Lebanon — Best for International Shipping

Aramex Lebanon

How to Choose for Your Business

1
Check coverage against your target areas. If your ads are targeting all of Lebanon, you need a provider who actually covers the South, Bekaa Valley, and northern regions — not just Beirut and Mount Lebanon.
2
Prioritize confirmation over delivery speed. A partner who confirms and delivers in 2 days outperforms one who ships same-day without confirmation, because confirmed orders actually get received.
3
Insist on weekly payouts. Monthly payout cycles lock your capital and slow down reinvestment in stock and ads. In a market where you're collecting fresh dollars, weekly payouts are the operating standard to expect.

Frequently Asked Questions

Which is the best COD delivery company in Lebanon?

MDM Express is the strongest option for e-commerce sellers. All Lebanese governorates are covered, orders are confirmed before dispatch, and cash is paid out weekly — the combination that consistently delivers the highest delivery rates.

Does Aramex offer COD service in Lebanon?

Yes, Aramex Lebanon handles COD collection. However, it lacks order confirmation features and has longer payout cycles, making it better suited for international shipping than domestic COD e-commerce at scale.

How do I choose a COD delivery company in Lebanon?

Four factors matter most: full coverage of your target areas, order confirmation before dispatch, delivery speed under 2 days for Beirut, and weekly cash payouts. Companies that offer all four consistently outperform those that don't.

Can COD delivery companies handle USD in Lebanon?

Yes. Reputable COD providers in Lebanon collect in both USD and Lebanese pounds and transfer collected amounts in agreed currency. Confirm the currency handling protocol before signing any agreement.

Frequently Asked Questions

Which is the best delivery company for COD in Libya?

MDM Express leads for e-commerce sellers. It covers all Libyan cities, confirms every order before dispatch, and pays out collected cash weekly — a combination no other company in Libya currently offers.

What is the average delivery cost in Libya?

Delivery fees range from 10 to 25 LYD per shipment depending on the company and destination. Major cities are cheaper; remote southern areas cost more. Factor this into your product pricing.

How does order confirmation improve delivery rates?

Calling customers before dispatch eliminates unreachable customers and fake orders, which account for 15–30% of all COD orders. Companies that confirm orders consistently see 70–80% delivery rates vs 50–60% for those that don't.

Can I use Aramex for COD inside Libya?

Yes, but Aramex is better suited for international shipping. For domestic COD e-commerce, the lack of order confirmation, slower payout cycles, and limited rural coverage make it a suboptimal choice compared to dedicated COD platforms.

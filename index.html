Contact Us
How quickly can I start selling on MDM Express? +
Most accounts go live within 48–72 hours of submitting your application. Our onboarding team handles everything from store integration to first shipment.
Do I need to hold inventory myself? +
No — for our Affiliate program you hold zero inventory. For Sellers, we warehouse and fulfill every order from our MENA facilities on your behalf.
Which markets do you currently cover? +
Libya and the Iraq are fully live. Lebanon, Qatar, Bahrain and Oman are launching soon — join the waitlist to be first.
How does Sourcing Advance work? +
We fund your inventory and freight costs upfront, paid directly to your suppliers. Repayment is auto-deducted as a small percentage of each weekly COD settlement — no equity, no bank.

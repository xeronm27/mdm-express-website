We use cookies to make MDM Express work, remember your preferences, and understand how sellers use our platform. This policy explains exactly what we use and how you can control it.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember information about your visit — such as your preferred language, login status, or items in a cart — making your next visit easier and the site more useful.

Cookies are not programs and cannot carry viruses or malware. They are simply small data files stored locally on your device.

Similar technologies such as web beacons, pixels, and local storage may also be used alongside cookies and are covered by this policy.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies Required

Necessary for the platform to function. They enable login sessions, security tokens, and core features. Cannot be disabled.

Functional Cookies Optional

Remember your preferences such as language, dashboard layout, and notification settings to personalise your experience.

Analytics Cookies Optional

Help us understand how sellers use the platform — which pages are most visited, where users drop off, and how features perform.

Marketing Cookies Optional

Used to show relevant content and measure the effectiveness of marketing campaigns. Only set with your consent.

Specific Cookies We Set

  • mdm_session — Maintains your login session. Essential. Expires at end of session.
  • mdm_csrf — Prevents cross-site request forgery attacks. Essential. Expires at end of session.
  • mdm_lang — Stores your preferred language. Functional. Expires after 1 year.
  • mdm_prefs — Stores dashboard preferences. Functional. Expires after 6 months.
  • mdm_analytics — Tracks page views and feature usage. Analytics. Expires after 90 days.
  • mdm_consent — Records your cookie consent choices. Essential. Expires after 1 year.

3. Third-Party Cookies

Some pages on our platform may include content or features from third parties that set their own cookies. These third parties have their own privacy and cookie policies which we do not control.

Third parties we may use include:

  • Google Analytics — Platform usage analytics (Google Privacy Policy)
  • Cloudflare — Performance, security, and CDN services
  • Intercom / support tools — Customer support chat functionality

You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

4. Managing Your Cookie Preferences

When you first visit MDM Express, you will be presented with a cookie consent banner that allows you to accept all cookies or customise your preferences. You can update your preferences at any time.

Browser Controls

You can also control cookies directly through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to:

  • View cookies stored on your device
  • Delete all or specific cookies
  • Block cookies from specific websites
  • Block all third-party cookies
  • Be notified before a cookie is set

Instructions for managing cookies in common browsers:

  • Google Chrome
  • Mozilla Firefox
  • Safari
  • Microsoft Edge

Please note that disabling essential cookies may prevent the platform from functioning correctly and may affect your ability to log in or use core features.

5. Do Not Track

Some browsers include a "Do Not Track" (DNT) signal that requests that websites not track your browsing activity. Currently, there is no consistent industry standard for responding to DNT signals. MDM Express does not currently respond to DNT signals but will continue to monitor developments in this area.
